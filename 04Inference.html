Implementation of Knowledge GraphsÂ¶

  • Graphs are sets of triples.

  • Each triple describes a relation between a pair of entities.

  • Similar to natural language, where sentences consist of subject, predicate, object.

  • Transformation from unstructured to structured data:

    • NLP (Natural Language Processing) is applied to determine subject,predicate,object

    • these elements are the triples, which constitute the graph
